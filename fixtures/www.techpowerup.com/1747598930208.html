Valkyrie V240 LITE Review

by
crazyeyesreaper
,
on May 16th, 2025,
in CPU Coolers.
Manufacturer: Valkyrie
Packaging & Contents »

Introduction


A big thank you to Valkyrie for providing the review sample!

Valkyrie is a relative newcomer in the DIY PC space, with the company launching numerous products within the last year. It features a full range of all-in-one liquid coolers, air coolers, and fans, all available in black or white. Suffice to say, they are on a roll. Add in a generous warranty, and you have a company worth watching to see where they go from here.


Today's review will focus on Valkyrie's all new V240 LITE all-in-one liquid cooler. Taking what they learned from the previous design, which was an exceptional performer, Valkyrie now is offering another interesting option that I have high hopes for. To start it uses a unique magnetic pump cover that features ARGB LED lighting along with en exposed circuit board, giving it a unique appearance that will likely be love it or hate it. Beyond that they are using a new fan design that has supposedly been optimized to provide the best performance possible on this closed loop cooler's radiator. Before we put those claims to the test let's take a closer look at the Valkyrie V240 LITE itself.
