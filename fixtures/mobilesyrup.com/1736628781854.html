Canadian Amazon Prime users can get two free Kindle books this month

Those who aren't with Prime can get the books for $1.99 each instead

Bradly Shankar@bradshankar
Jan 9, 20255:33 PM EST 9 comments

Amazon Canada is offering two free Kindle books to Prime members in January.

It’s part of Amazon’s First Reads promotion, which gives pre-release access to editor’s picks titles in various categories, including Short Story (Lee Childs’ Eleven Numbers), Family Drama (Danielle Prescord’s The Rules of Fortune) and Domestic Thriller (Tamara L. Miller’s Into the Fall). You can choose two of these books to receive for free. Those who aren’t Prime members can get these books for $1.99 each.

Check out all of the First Reads books here.

An Amazon Prime membership costs $9.99/month or $99.99/year.
