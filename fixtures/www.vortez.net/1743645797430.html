DeepCool Launches CH690 DIGITAL With Built-in Real-Time System Monitoring

👤by Michael Pabia  Comments 📅31.03.2025 16:56:46


DeepCool announced the launch of the CH690 DIGITAL, a next-generation PC case that combines real-time system monitoring, customizable cooling, and stunning aesthetics for high-performance builds. Designed for gamers and professionals alike, the CH690 DIGITAL delivers exceptional airflow, broad compatibility, and innovative features to meet the demands of modern PC enthusiasts.


Side Display Panel


The DeepCool CH690 DIGITAL features an integrated digital display that provides instant access to vital system statistics, including CPU, GPU, and PSU temperature (°C/°F), power consumption, usage, and frequency. With switchable modes, users can effortlessly monitor fan speeds and system performance, transforming the case into a high-tech command center for optimal efficiency.

Engineered for superior thermal performance, the DeepCool CH690 DIGITAL supports extensive cooling configurations, including radiators up to 420mm on top, 280mm on the side, and 360mm within the PSU shroud. Its mesh PSU shroud enables vertical airflow, while multiple fan configurations ensure efficient heat dissipation. The case accommodates 450mm GPUs, 180mm PSUs, and CPU air coolers up to 182mm, making it ideal for high-end builds.

With a semi-glass side panel, the DeepCool CH690 DIGITAL offers a stunning view of internal components while maintaining a clean and sturdy design. For enhanced airflow, the rear panel can be replaced with a mesh panel and fan bracket. The integrated light strip slot allows for personalized RGB lighting, creating an immersive gaming atmosphere. Additional features include an adjustable GPU holder, a front I/O panel with USB 3.0, USB Type-C, and an audio jack, and a magnetic bottom air filter for easy maintenance.

Availability, Warranty, and Pricing
The DeepCool CH690 DIGITAL case is available in two editions: black (MSRP: $99) and white (MSRP: $109.99). It is backed by a one-year warranty and will be available through the DeepCool worldwide network of authorized retailers and distributors.
